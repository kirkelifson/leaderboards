About us

What we are

A free open-source Source mod based in Counter-Strike: Source. Focused on bunny-hopping and surf mechanics from said game, with global leader-boards to compete for the best times, and includes many fixes to the gameplay to make your experience more consistent.
We're still in development!

Features

Built-in timer. Global leaderboards. Fixes to the gamemode & gameplay.

The team

Led by Source speedrunner Gocnak we're a group of volunteers who spend free time developing the mod. You can view the credits page here

We're in active development

You can check what we're doing in our GitHub repo or what we're planning to do on our Trello board.

Momentum Mod FAQ

What is Momentum Mod?

Momentum Mod is a free stand-alone Source mod with Counter-Strike: Source physics dedicated to bhop and surfing while trying to improve them and simultaneously making it easier to access and play (offline and online) as well as being cheaper than any other Source game you'd have to buy to play either of these game modes.

How much will the game cost?

Free.

When is Momentum Mod coming out?

The mod is currently being worked on with no realistic ETA in sight. We want to keep people informed about progress! Check what we're planning on our Trello board.
