About

As Momentum Mod is still early in it's development timeline, there are not a lot of confirmed features we can share. We can promise Momentum Mod will offer everything surf and bhop servers have and more, and will be free to play!

If you want to see what we are working on, visit our Trello page, Github organization, or dev blog.

If you would like to contribute to the project, feel free to contact us. We are currently looking for C++ programmers, artists, and level designers that have some sort of experience. We are not in need of official playtesters at the current stage of development, but you are free to try out the mod at the install page.
